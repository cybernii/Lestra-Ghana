About us

OFFICE CLEANING

L’extra recognizes that the presentation of your front office is fundamental to creating an excellent first impression and we also understand that a refreshing back office environment is key to productivity.

 

Office cleaning schedules include routine and one-off cleaning services and with a wide range of schedules to choose from, we fit into your time and convenience.

 

Our trained personnel make sure your office setting is kept fresh all day long with our stand by services.

We are sure to leave a smile on your face as we exit your doorway.

EVENT CLEANING

Events generate high traffic and require a deep but fast clean to ensure that the event space can return to normal use in the shortest time possible. Our event cleaning service ensures that your venue is back in action in a very short space of time. Even as the event is ongoing, we make sure to keep the venue free of litter all the way.

 

Lextra’s event cleaning service takes care of events such as stadia events, music festivals/carnivals, parties, wedding venue, conferences, and other events alike.

 

We provide professional, uniformed event cleaners for small, medium and large scale event cleaning before, during and after the event.

SHOWROOM CLEANING

Cleanliness and an attractive appearance will leave your showroom customers with an amazing first impression.

 

Having a clean environment demonstrates a high-quality, high-value showroom and allows customers to focus on the items and artwork being displayed.

 

Our cleaners and staff work diligently to get your showroom into impeccable shape and to maintain its appearance with every service visit.
