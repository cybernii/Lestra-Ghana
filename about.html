About L'extra

About Us

LGL is your national one-stop shop full service facility maintenance company. Operating 24/7 L’extra serves as a single source solution for every maintenance requirement and offering integrated services across the full property and infrastructure life cycle.

OUR prime aim is to preserve an edifice in its initial state, as far as practicable while retaining, where appropriate, its investment values, and presenting a good appearance; so that it effectively serves its purpose and ensure it remains as impressive in the future as they were first launched.

 

At L’extra, we never provide a predetermined set of solutions to a challenge. Instead, we develop a bespoke offer that responds directly to our customers’ needs and ensures the right people are involved at every step along the way. To support this, we have nurtured an agile corporate culture that enables us to respond quickly and intelligently to change.

 

At the center of our business is client focus and We proudly offer a comprehensive partnership of professional service and communication to our clientele , strengthened by an unending commitment to maintain and improve our clients facilities. We are able to provide clients with peace of mind and ensure their business will continue runnings smoothly and efficiently around-the-clock as well as a peaceful, comfortable and safe home while our professionals all the while defining industry’s best practices.

 

Employees are a vital component in providing our unique services , and having the best people at hand is of course crucial to success in management and maintenance services; something We secure with employee development through means such as training and motivation to extract the best possible customer service from them.

Vision

  1. To be the nation’s leading Facilities Services Company, dedicated to providing genuinely pleasant satisfying and quality maintenance services to meet the customer and his facility needs.
  2. To create top of the line service by gaining insights into consumer needs. Providing innovative, industry leading solutions across all customer categories.
  3. To recognize and reward excellence, providing opportunities that encourage individuals to maximize their potential.
  4. To be a one-stop-shop for domestic and commercial facility maintenance for facilities of any size and in any part of the country

Mission

To provide quality service in an efficient and professional manner to ensure building safety and comfort.

What we do

We offer total facilities management solutions to commercial, residential, hospitality, industrial, education and retail projects… From core engineering services such as the complex building maintenance, to a range of soft services including cleaning of offices and manning receptions, we assure our clients’ peace of mind as their operations run seamlessly in their environment.

 

On every given day, your building has several facility problems that arise A single facility encounters a broad range of maintenance issues every day, week, month and year. Regular preventive maintenance helps maintain a clean consistent appearance in your locations whiles optimizing functionality.

 

Because the unexpected is bound to happen, we are always here and ready to fix all arising problems. Multiply those problems across the nation and the daunting challenge of managing maintenance issues becomes apparent. That’s where L’extra comes in. We are a facility maintenance company that takes the absolute care and upkeep of your edifice at heart. We streamline the maintenance process with individual accounts on combined services
