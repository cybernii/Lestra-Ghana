About Us

LGL is your national one-stop shop full service facility maintenance company. Operating 24/7 L’extra serves as a single source solution for every maintenance requirement and offering integrated services across the full property and infrastructure life cycle.

OUR prime aim is to preserve an edifice in its initial state, as far as practicable while retaining, where appropriate, its investment values, and presenting a good appearance; so that it effectively serves its purpose and ensure it remains as impressive in the future as they were first launched.

 

At L’extra, we never provide a predetermined set of solutions to a challenge. Instead, we develop a bespoke offer that responds directly to our customers’ needs and ensures the right people are involved at every step along the way. To support this, we have nurtured an agile corporate culture that enables us to respond quickly and intelligently to change.

 

At the center of our business is client focus and We proudly offer a comprehensive partnership of professional service and communication to our clientele , strengthened by an unending commitment to maintain and improve our clients facilities. We are able to provide clients with peace of mind and ensure their business will continue runnings smoothly and efficiently around-the-clock as well as a peaceful, comfortable and safe home while our professionals all the while defining industry’s best practices.
